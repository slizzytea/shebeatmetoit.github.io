Mystic Lakers Escape With Double-Overtime Win Over Ballers

December 8, 2025

The She Beat Me To It Ballers were involved in an instant classic on Monday night, falling to the Mystic Lakers 31–30 in a dramatic double-overtime battle. The game featured clutch shots, tough defense, and constant momentum swings.

Regulation ended with both teams tied after the Ballers fought back late behind strong play from Aiden and Jack, who combined to lead the offense. Neither team was able to pull away in the first overtime as both defenses stepped up.

In the second overtime, the game came down to the final moments. Mystic Lakers guard Jacob, who finished with 16 points, stepped to the free-throw line with the game on the line.

Jacob calmly knocked down the game-winning free throw, giving the Lakers a narrow victory and ending the Ballers’ comeback attempt. The loss was a tough one, but the Ballers showed resilience and composure throughout.

Despite the defeat, the performance proved that the Ballers can compete in high-pressure situations. The experience gained from the double-overtime thriller will only help the team moving forward.
