December 8, 2025 Ballers Dominate Benchwarmers Elite in Season Opener

Ballers Dominate Benchwarmers Elite in Season Opener

The She Beat Me To It Ballers opened their season with a statement win, defeating Benchwarmers Elite 58–15 at home. From the opening tip, the Ballers set the tone with strong defense and fast-paced offense.

Aiden and Jack led the way early, pushing the tempo and scoring in transition. Tom had a big night, finishing with 12 points and earning Player of the Game honors for his effort on both ends of the floor.

The Ballers never let up, sharing the ball and getting contributions from the entire roster. The dominant win gave the team confidence as they moved into their second game of the night.

December 8, 2025 Mystic Lakers Edge Ballers in Double Overtime Thriller

Mystic Lakers Edge Ballers in Double Overtime Thriller

The second game of the night turned into an instant classic as the She Beat Me To It Ballers battled the Mystic Lakers through regulation and two overtime periods.

Aiden and Jack once again paced the offense, while Tom and William provided key scoring during clutch moments. The game remained tight until the final seconds of double overtime.

Mystic Lakers guard Jacob finished with 16 points and sealed the game by knocking down the game-winning free throw. The Lakers escaped with a 31–30 victory, handing the Ballers a tough but hard-fought loss.
